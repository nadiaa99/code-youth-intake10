Alissa Holder

Alissa is an author, mother and creative. A New York City native, she earned her BA from the University at Albany with a major in Sociology and a minor in Education. She has been an Early Childhood educator for over 12 years. She has a passion for fashion, wardrobe styling, crafting and working with children.
